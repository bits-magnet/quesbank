Question:1
Complete the following table and verify Euler's formula in each case.
 
 
Faces (F) 6 4 9 7
Edges (E) 12 6 16 15
Vertex (V) 8 4 9 10
Solution:
  I   II III IV
Faces (F) 6 4 9 7
Edges (E) 12 6 16 15
Vertices (V) 8 4 9 10
Euler's formula (F − E + V) 6 − 12 + 8 = 2 4 − 6 + 4 = 2 9 − 16 + 9 = 2 7 − 15 + 10 = 2
Hence Euler's formula is verified for these figures.
