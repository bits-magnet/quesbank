Question:17
Find the value of n when:

(i) 52n × 53 = 59
(ii) 8 × 2n+2 = 32
(iii) 62n+1 ÷ 36 = 63
Solution:
(i) 52n × 53 = 59
     52n+3 = 59          [since an × am = am+n]

     On equating the coefficients:
     2n + 3 = 9
     ⇒ 2n = 9 − 3
     ⇒ 2n = 6
     ∴ n =62=3

(ii) 8 × 2n+2 = 32
     ⇒ (2)3 × 2n+2 = (2)5      [since 23 = 8 and 25 = 32]
     ⇒ (2)3+ (n+2) = (2)5 
     On equating the coefficients:
      3 + n + 2 = 5
      ⇒ n + 5 = 5
      ⇒ n = 5 − 5
      ∴ n = 0

(iii) 62n+1 ÷ 36 = 63
       ⇒ 62n+1 ÷ 62 = 63       [since 36 = 62]
       ⇒ 62n+162=63
       ⇒ 62n+1-2=63          [since aman=am-n ]
       ⇒ 62n-1 = 63
        On equating the coefficients:
        2n - 1 = 3
        ⇒ 2n = 3 + 1
        ⇒ 2n = 4
         ∴ n = 42=2
